Our Services

At NF QUALITY MANAGEMENT INSTITUTE LTD we offer a variety of services including

1. Advisory services

- To Private Companies, SMEs, and publics institutions on access to quality through the implementation of products and system standards.

- Assistance to new Hotels and restaurants process an RDB operating License

- Advisory services and Technical Assistance in Star rating and classification of Hotels, Appartments, restaurants and Lodges

2. Organise and facilitate Training on different standards:

RS ISO 9001:2015 Quality management System

RS IS 19011:2018 Guidelines for Auditing Management Systems

RS ISO/IEC 17015:2017 General Requirements for the Competence of Testing and Calibration Laboratories

RS ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health and Safety Management System

RS ISO 14001:2015 Environmental management system

RS ISO 184:2017 Food Safety based on Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) - Recommendation for any organization in the food chain

RS ISO 22000:2018 Food Safety Management System-Requirements
